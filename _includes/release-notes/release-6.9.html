Changes released February 23, 2018 (USAJOBS Release 6.9)

This release of USAJOBS focused on updating the hiring paths.

We made the following changes:

1. Added new hiring paths, split existing paths

We took on feedback from multiple agencies and OPM Policy and have revised our list of hiring paths. There are 3 new sub-paths and 3 paths that have been split.
