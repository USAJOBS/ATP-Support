ATP has been redesigned to support multiple workflows and to embrace integrated features as they become available.

Resume Mining

ATP’s most used feature is Resume Mining, which allows HR users and hiring managers to discover qualified job seekers who have made their resumes searchable in their USAJOBS account. Resume Mining can be accessed from anywhere in ATP by clicking the “Search” button. You can start by:

  1. Searching nearly one million up-to-date resumes
  2. Finding interesting job seekers
  3. Creating a campaign from the search results
  4. Then, sharing the campaign with your recruitment team

Or you can create a campaign, add your colleagues, establish a framework for reviewing job seekers, and then search for talent. The choice of method is up to you and your recruitment team.

Campaigns

Campaigns are a new feature which allows for recruitment teams to share lists of job seekers and collaborate together when reviewing job seekers.
